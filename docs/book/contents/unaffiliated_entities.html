Items

Cannibal Whetstone

Take 1 hour to apply [sharpened] to a [bladed] weapon. Whetstone must be soaked in blood for 1 hour before use. Inflict 1 [bleed] on half crit. Gain 1 health at the beginning of your turn as long as the opponent is bleeding.
A warm mass of striated stone writhing almost imperceptibly. It bears a closed eye on one side. It scrapes against your hand. It screams in your head.

Footsoldier’s Whetstone

Takes 30 minutes to apply [sharpened] to a [bladed] weapon. Ignore 3 armor. Rolling a crit fail removes [sharpened].
A fragment of a relief etched into heavy white stone. The relief depicts a phalanx of soldiers in formation bearing partisans against an unseen force. The relief is etched deeply with identical runes in various styles.

Valiikon’s Whetstone

Takes 30 minutes to apply [sharpened]. Inflict 1 poison on hit. Inflict 1 poison if you do 2 poison in one turn. Missing an attack removes [sharpened].
